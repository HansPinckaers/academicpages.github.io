Decaffeinated coffee beans — sources in The Netherlands

hanspinckaers updated 7 days ago created: 24 August 2020
Coffee Personal Public

Single origin, freshly roast

All of them are decaffeinated using the Swiss Water-based decaffeination process except when otherwise mentioned. Also see the Product offering of Swiss Water that sells to roasters.

Favorite: my current favorite for in a cappucino is the Colombia single-origin from Brandzaak.

  • Peru
    • Daves
    • Blommers
      • Some nice acidity, but lacked a bit of flavour in a cappucino
    • Fascino Coffee
    • Giraffe
      • Light nuts (hazelnut), sugary but not overly sweet
    • Simon & Levelt | CO2 decaffeination
  • Colombia
    • Charlies Coffee Maestricht
    • Brandzaak
      • Dark chocolate, quite sweet
      • Note: they ship very fast after roasting, best taste after four days imo.
    • Fascino Coffee | Sugarcane decaf / Ethyl Acetate
    • Badeta
    • Brazuca coffee (email them to be sure)
    • Corio | CO2 decaffeination
    • Doppio | CO2 decaffeination
  • Guatemala
    • Fascino Coffee
      • Tastes pretty much what I says on the box: chocolate, nutty flavor, also sometimes a bit of raisins.
    • Single Estate
  • Brazil
    • Brazuca coffee (email them to be sure)
  • Nicaragua
    • Het Hoofdkwartier
  • Ethiopia
    • Keikoffie (probably ethyl acetate)
    • Bocca | CO2 decaffeination
      • Didn’t work for my in a cappuccino. Not enough flavour. Does have some acidity.
