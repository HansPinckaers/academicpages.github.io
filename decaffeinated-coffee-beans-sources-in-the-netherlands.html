Cafeïnevrije / decaf koffiebonen — verkrijgbaar in Nederland

All of them are decaffeinated using the Swiss Water-based decaffeination process except when otherwise mentioned. Also see the Product Offerings of Swiss Water that sells to roasters.

If you find another decaf roasters in The Netherlands, please email me, so I can keep this list up to date. My latest Google Search + update: March, 05 - 2021

Favorites

  1. The delicious chocolaty beans from Colombia single-origin from Brandzaak.
  2. The fruity Ethiopia beans from Fascino Coffee
